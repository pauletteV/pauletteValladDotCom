ABOUT

I am a passionate, self-motivated and detail oriented web designer/developer. I have 3 years of experience developing banner ads/websites for multiple national and regional brands. I am an abstract and organized thinker that can bring efficiency, creativity and professionalism to any project. Prior to changing careers in 2014, I have 11 years of experience working as operations support in finance and logistics. Drawing from my varied business background I have strong skills in communication, problem solving and organization. I am a hard working digital media creative with a well-rounded background that enables me to understand the needs of various clients.

Born and raised in South East Michigan, I am lover the arts in all of its glorious forms. In my spare time I love to cook, read, hike and listen to music. I enjoy traveling and the occasional spontaneous road trip! I also have an affinity for vintage objects such as clothing, jewelry and décor.
